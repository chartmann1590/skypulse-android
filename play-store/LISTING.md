## Full description  *(max 4000 chars)*

```
Ever looked up at a plane and wondered where it's going? SkyPulse turns your phone into a personal radar, showing the aircraft flying above you right now — in real time, on a live map.

Powered by community ADS-B data, SkyPulse tracks thousands of flights so you can watch them move across the sky, tap any plane for live details, and get notified the moment something interesting flies nearby.

★ LIVE FLIGHT MAP
See real-time aircraft positions on a smooth, interactive map. Planes move as they fly, so you can follow the traffic overhead, around your local airport, or anywhere you pan to.

★ NEARBY AIRCRAFT
Instantly see what's flying near you, ranked by distance, altitude or speed. Each aircraft shows its callsign, type and how far away it is — perfect for plane spotting from your backyard.

★ TAP FOR LIVE DETAILS
Tap any aircraft to open a live detail card with altitude, ground speed, heading, vertical rate and last-seen time. Watch the numbers update as the flight climbs, cruises or descends.

★ AIRPORTS AROUND YOU
Browse the airports nearest to you and jump straight into their arrivals, departures and live activity. A built-in airport database means it works the moment you open the app.

★ SMART FLIGHT ALERTS
Never miss the flights that matter. Set custom push alerts for:
• An aircraft entering your area
• A specific callsign appearing
• Low-altitude flights nearby
• Activity at a chosen airport
• A saved flight departing or landing

★ SAVE WHAT YOU LOVE
Bookmark aircraft, airports and areas for quick access, and keep an eye on the flights, fields and regions you care about most.

★ BUILT FOR EVERY SPOTTER
Whether you're an aviation enthusiast, a frequent flyer tracking a friend's plane, a photographer chasing the perfect shot, or just curious about that jet overhead — SkyPulse makes the sky readable.

WHY SKYPULSE?
• Real-time, map-based flight tracking
• Clean, modern, dark-themed design
• No account or sign-up required
• Lightweight and fast
• Works great for plane spotting and aviation fans

A NOTE ON COVERAGE
SkyPulse uses free, community-powered ADS-B networks. Coverage is excellent in most populated areas but can vary by region and altitude. Some military and private aircraft choose not to broadcast and won't appear. SkyPulse is intended for informational and entertainment purposes and should never be used for navigation or any safety-critical decisions.

Download SkyPulse and discover the world flying above you. Look up — the sky has never been this easy to read.
```
*(~2,180 characters — well under the 4,000 limit)*

## Data safety (Play Console → App content → Data safety)

SkyPulse requires no account/login. Declare based on the app's actual behavior:

- **Location** — *Approximate & precise location* used for the live map, nearby
  aircraft and location-based alerts. Processed on-device / sent to ADS-B data
  providers only to fetch nearby aircraft. Not used for advertising tracking by
  the app itself.
- **Analytics / Crash data** — Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics & Performance
  collect app activity, diagnostics and device identifiers.
- **Advertising** — Google AdMob (with the Google UMP consent flow) may collect
  device/advertising identifiers for ads.
- Review Firebase & AdMob SDK disclosures when filling the questionnaire.
